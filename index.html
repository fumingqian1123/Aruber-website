Aruber Biotechnology Co., Ltd. specializes in extracting bio-therapeutic or bio-applicable molecules, especially pigments, from highly effective probiotic bacteria, providing natural, safe, and environmentally friendly new materials.

The company utilizes low-cost substrates to convert into high-valued molecules such as carotenoids and other antioxidants, offering non-toxic and harmless materials to industries with high standards for pigment additives, such as the cosmetics and food processing industries. Currently, the company has successfully extracted diverse pigments, with mature technology ready for using in the market.

Technical Highlights

1. Precision Strain Selection: We meticulously select high-yield probiotic strains from both natural environments or humans. Through non-genetically approaches, we enhance their material-producing capabilities, creating highly efficient "new material factories."

2. Intelligent Fermentation Control: In customized cultural tanks, we precisely regulate parameters such as temperature and pH, combined with scientifically balanced carbon-nitrogen nutrient bases, to achieve large-scale, high-yield pigment production by probiotics.

3. Efficient and Green Extraction: We employ extraction, ultrasonic disruption, and other physical or chemical techniques to efficiently separate new materials from the cultural broth.

4. Multi-stage Precision Purification: Through various processes and steps, we purify and refine new materials containing impurities, enhancing their purity and stability.

5. Strict Quality Control and Delivery: We test for material contents, heavy metals, probiotic activities, and other indicators, supplying finished products in customized forms for use in food, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and other industries.
